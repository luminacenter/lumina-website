🏛️ The Luminarch Center – Project FAQ

📌 Overview

The Luminarch Center is a nonprofit organization based in Liberia and North Carolina, focused on educational empowerment, community transformation, and holistic support for underserved children and families.

🎯 Vision

To illuminate the potential in every child and family through education, nutrition, mentorship, and faith-based values.

🏢 Facilities Breakdown

  • 1st Floor – Cafeteria: Serves free breakfast and dinner to 250 children daily
  • 2nd Floor – Tech Lab: 100 computers, coding, tutoring
  • 3rd Floor – Library: Books, reading programs, study hub
  • Rooftop: Outdoor classroom and community space

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is The Luminarch Center? A nonprofit serving underserved children and families.
  • Who do you serve? Youth and families in need, especially in Liberia.
  • Is it faith-based? Yes, inspired by Christian values but open to all.
  • Are services free? Yes – meals, tech, support, and education are free.
  • How can I volunteer? Email or visit our site for opportunities.
  • Can I donate or sponsor a program? Yes – we welcome both.
